PENUT(peer-to-peer E-commerce Network for User Trading)

Penut is a peer-to-peer e-commerce network that offers an inclusive and user- friendly platform for online trading. With Penut, everyone, including lower organizations, can post and buy products without any transaction fees. Penut allows buyers and sellers to connect directly through the platform to negotiate the price and payment method. This approach offers more flexibility in transactions and helps to avoid additional transaction fees.
