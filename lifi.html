LIFI Technology(seminar)

LiFi, which stands for "Light Fidelity," is a wireless communication technology that uses visible light, infrared, and ultraviolet light to transmit data. Unlike traditional wireless communication methods, such as Wi-Fi, which use radio waves, LiFi utilizes the visible light spectrum for data transmission.LiFi offers the potential for extremely high data transmission speeds, often surpassing the capabilities of conventional Wi-Fi. This is because visible light has a much higher frequency and bandwidth than radio waves.
